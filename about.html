About
A collection of websites, projects, and artworks.

Greetings and welcome to my website.

My name is Stephen Wike and I am the author/creator for the projects showcased on this website. I've also been involved in many development and artistic industries across a wide domain of platforms and services. I have experience in two primary domains; the Arts and Software.

I started my adventure with an education pursuing a degree in Fine Arts with an emphasis in 3D Animation. 3D Animation being a technical-fueled industry, I began learning code to compliment my school projects. The core of my education exposed me to the graphical competencies including design, illustration, animation, and 3D graphics modeling.

While completing my bachelors of Fine Arts, I began pursuing a secondary bachelors in Computer Science and Engineering. These courses expanded those core competencies to include software development skills, database scheming and administration, algorithms and heuristics, and networking.

As I was nearing completion of the second bachelor in Computer Science and Engineering, I obtaining my first internship working with medical devices and implementing internal software tools for diagnosing medical device procedures. This led me to a job offer with the company as a software developer within the company's Research and Development (R&D) working on enterprise software.

Currently, I'm working on the proofing of our enterprise software systems architecture as our business is pushing DevOps strategies into our value stream. These include micro-service deployments along with integration strategies supported and managed by container-orchestrated clusters.
